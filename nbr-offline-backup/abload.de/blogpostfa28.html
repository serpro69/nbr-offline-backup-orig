Donation page in English/Spendenseite auf Englisch

geschrieben von Jens, 07.06.2014 11:39 Uhr

Liebe Abloader,
Dear Abloader,

diese Information richtet sich nur an unsere englischsprachigen Nutzer.
This information aims at our English speaking users only.

In the past years, we have frequently been asked to also enable our non-German users to support us through voluntary contributions and to therefore translate our donation page into English. Good news: We have now set up everything, experiencing increasing numbers of foreign visitors we want to welcome warmly at this point. :-)

In addition to translating our donation page, we have also ensured worldwide compatibility regarding our payment methods. From now on, you are able to donate via direct debit, bank / wire transfer, PayPal, and mail / post. Please do always mind that there might be transactions fees coming up which you should investigate before - at least if you are from outside Europe.

Do you want to know more about our "Currywurst Concept"? Click here: http://abload.de/spenden_en.php

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us: http://www.abload.de/contact.php?lang=en

Regards from Bremerhaven,
Jens
