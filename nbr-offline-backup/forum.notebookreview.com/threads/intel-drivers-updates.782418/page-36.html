    I think there are now two different Microsoft Store Intel control panels.

    Intel Graphics Control Panel
    Intel Graphics Command Center

    The latter is newer and is slated to replace the Control Panel, but still in beta form.

    I couldn't get the regular Control Panel to work for a while, then it suddenly started working after the last couple of updates (UHD630 on my desktop). Annoyingly, that same older DCH driver that broke the Control Panel also broke Quicksync. This made ASIC video encoding and decoding impossible, and reverting the drivers didn't work (since the DCH drivers left a lot of stuff that isn't removed by any, and I mean ANY, driver uninstall/cleaning/purging, etc). Luckily, someone found the exact files to remove.

    Either way, it's all in the past now. I also upgraded my desktop GPU to something Turing based and have never touched Intel's Quicksync since.
